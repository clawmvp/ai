# Privacy Policy for Solana Tabla Pro

**Effective Date**: February 2, 2026

## Introduction

Solana Tabla Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Blockchain Data
- **Public Wallet Address**: We access your Solana wallet public key when you connect via Mobile Wallet Adapter
- **On-Chain Transactions**: Game results, bets, and NFT purchases are recorded on the Solana blockchain and are publicly visible
- **Game History**: Match results and tournament participation stored on-chain

### 2. Usage Data
- **Game Statistics**: Number of games played, wins/losses, ELO rating
- **WebSocket Connections**: Temporary session data for real-time multiplayer (deleted after disconnection)
- **Device Information**: Mobile device type and OS version (for compatibility purposes only)

### 3. Data We Do NOT Collect
- ❌ Private keys or seed phrases
- ❌ Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts or media files
- ❌ Advertising identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

- **Game Functionality**: Matchmaking, leaderboards, tournament management
- **Anti-Cheat**: Detecting and preventing cheating or abuse
- **Technical Support**: Debugging issues and improving app performance
- **Analytics**: Understanding app usage patterns (anonymized data only)

## Data Storage

- **On-Chain Data**: Permanently stored on Solana blockchain (immutable)
- **Off-Chain Data**: Temporarily stored on our backend servers for active game sessions only
- **Retention**: Off-chain session data is deleted within 24 hours of inactivity

## Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:
- **Solana Blockchain**: Public blockchain for transactions
- **Mobile Wallet Adapter**: Wallet connection protocol (no data sent to us)
- **Metaplex**: NFT minting standard

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: View your on-chain data via Solana blockchain explorers
- **Deletion**: Contact us to request deletion of off-chain cached data
- **Opt-Out**: Disconnect your wallet to stop using the app at any time

## Children's Privacy

Solana Tabla Pro is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted WebSocket connections (WSS)
- No storage of private keys
- Rate limiting and anti-spam protections
- Regular security audits

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
